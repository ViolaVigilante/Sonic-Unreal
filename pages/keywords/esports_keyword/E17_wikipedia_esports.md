    <p>The goal: To reach a specific page on Wikipedia, starting on an unrelated page and only clicking on hyperlinks within the text. No using the search bar, no Ctrl+F searching the text on the page, and no clicking on links anywhere outside the main body of the text. Complete this challenge faster than your opponents (in terms of either time elapsed or number of links clicked), and you win!</p>
    <p>“Wikipedia Esports” is something of a misnomer, as there is none of the institutionalized structure for competition that one might find in esports like <a class="popup-hyperlink" data-bs-toggle="popover" data-bs-html="true" data-bs-title="Explore Further" data-bs-content="<a href='/Sonic-Unreal/league-of-legends'><em>League of Legends</em></a>"><em>League of Legends</em></a> or <a class="popup-hyperlink" data-bs-toggle="popover" data-bs-html="true" data-bs-title="Explore Further" data-bs-content="<a href='/Sonic-Unreal/valorant'><em>Valorant</em></a>"><em>Valorant</em></a> - and, indeed, the term “Wikipedia Esports” is my own invention. Nevertheless, it (more-or-less) meets <a class="popup-hyperlink" data-bs-toggle="popover" data-bs-html="true" data-bs-title="Explore Further" data-bs-content="<a href='/Sonic-Unreal/esports'>Esports</a>"> our main criteria for an esport</a> - <em>“the competitive play of video games within externally organized infrastructures for competition that go beyond the game itself.”</em> While it may be a stretch to call Wikipedia a “video game,” this practice is otherwise clearly recognizable as the competitive use of interactive digital media within an externally-imposed set of rules and regulations (and there is plenty of <a class="popup-hyperlink" data-bs-toggle="popover" data-bs-html="true" data-bs-title="Explore Further" data-bs-content="<a href='/Sonic-Unreal/esports-and-as-video-games'>Esports and/as Video Games</a>"> precedent for esports outside of video games</a>). What I am calling “Wikipedia Esports” - a tongue-in-cheek label meant to draw attention to the similarities of this practice to the other esports discussed in this project - comes out of <a class="popup-hyperlink" data-bs-toggle="popover" data-bs-html="true" data-bs-title="Explore Further" data-bs-content="<a href='/Sonic-Unreal/speedrunning'>Speedrunning</a>"> speedrunning communities</a> rather than esports proper, though there are significant overlaps between these communities and speedrunning would fall within <a class="popup-hyperlink" data-bs-toggle="popover" data-bs-html="true" data-bs-title="Explore Further" data-bs-content="<a href='/Sonic-Unreal/esports'>Esports</a>"> the capacious framework for esports I propose in this project</a>.</p>
